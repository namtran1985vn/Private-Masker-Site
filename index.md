# Private Masker – Face Privacy Protection for Videos

**Automatically detect and mask faces in your videos with AI that runs entirely on your device.**

Private Masker uses advanced on-device AI to detect and blur faces in your videos. Protect privacy, share safely, and stay anonymous—without ever uploading to the cloud. **Your videos stay private. Always.**

---

## What Private Masker Does

### Automatic face detection and masking
Detect and blur faces in your videos automatically using on-device AI. Perfect for sharing videos while protecting everyone's privacy.

### Remove metadata for untraceable sharing
Strip location, device info, and timestamps from your videos. Share without leaving a digital trail.

### Voice protection with pitch modification
Change voice pitch to make speech unrecognizable while keeping words understandable. Great for interviews or conversations.

### Multiple export resolutions
Choose from 480p to 4K resolution. Free users get 480p; upgrade to Pro for higher quality exports.

## Privacy Options Explained

### Untraceable Mode
Removes metadata like location, device info, timestamps, and camera data. Recommended for maximum privacy when sharing publicly.

### Voice Protection
Modifies voice pitch to make speakers harder to identify while keeping speech understandable. Perfect for interviews or sensitive conversations.

### Prevent Quality Loss for Social Posting
Optimizes video encoding for social media uploads. Helps maintain detail when platforms recompress your video.

### Boost Face Detection
Runs face detection more frequently for fast-moving subjects. Takes longer but catches more faces in sports, dancing, or action videos.

## FAQ

**Do you upload my videos?**
No. Private Masker processes everything locally on your device.

**Does Private Masker require internet?**
No. It works fully offline.

**What does Private Masker do?**
It detects and blurs faces in videos, removes metadata, and optionally modifies voice pitch—all on-device.

**Do you collect user data?**
No. We do not collect or store personal data or videos.

**What's the difference between Free and Pro?**
Free users get 480p export with basic face masking. Pro users unlock up to 4K resolution, untraceable mode, voice protection, and advanced features.
